D · User journeys
Three visitors, three paths

The first-timer

Home → experience the signs → Concept → Craft → curiosity → a lane → case → next case → Contact

Feels the design before seeing a single list. The story sells the archive.

The recruiter / CD

any page → top-right menu → lane → case — 2 clicks to any project → next-case loop keeps them in

Zero friction to proof. The menu is always there; no forced storytelling.

The returner

arrives with a case URL → case → lane index one tap away → Contact cap on every page

Every page is a complete front door: mark → home, menu → lanes, cap → contact.

E · Navigation rules
The contract
01

Story upstairs, archive downstairs

The 3 C’s live on Home and hold zero project lists. The three lanes hold every project exactly once. The C’s point, the lanes contain.

02

Lanes are treatments

Campaigns = black, cinematic. Branding = white paper, X-frames. Work = frames & film. You always know where you are by the light.

03

Two clicks to anything

Persistent bar: mark → home, three lanes + Contact on the right. Any project from any page in two clicks; case-to-case in one.

04

One destination

Contact is an end-cap, not a maze: the same single CTA closes Home, every lane and every case. One email, one voice.
